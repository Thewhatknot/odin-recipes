CANDIED YAMS


Ingredients


  1. 5 to 6 medium sweet potatoes (3-4 lbs)
  2. 1 cup of Granulated Sugar
  3. 3 cups of Brown Sugar
  4. 1 stick of Salted Butter
  5. 2 tbsp of Water
  6. 2 tbsp of Ground Cinnamon
  7. 1/2 tsp of All Spice
  8. 2 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  9. 2 tsp of Lemmon Juice

Instructions

  • Clean and peel sweet potatoes.
  • Cut sweet potatoes into slices then cut slices in halves.
  • Melt butter in pot.
  • Add in sugar, brown sugar, and water. Mix over medium heat until it boils.
  • Add sweet potatoes to pot and evenly coat with mixture.
  • Stir in cinnamon, salt, all spice, vanilla extract and lemmon juice.
  • Place lid slightly off pot so it's not fully covered.
  • Cook for 20 to 25 minutes.
